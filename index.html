üë®‚Äçüç≥ Meal Preparation Process

Step 1: Preparation (10 minutes)

We washed all the vegetables carefully. Then we cut a few carrots into small pieces and chopped some onions. The chicken was cut into many small portions.

Step 2: Rice Cooking (20 minutes)

We cooked some rice in a rice cooker. We used enough water but not too much. A little salt was added for flavor.

Step 3: Stir-frying (15 minutes)

We heated a little oil in a pan. First, we cooked the chicken until all pieces were golden. Then we added all the vegetables and stir-fried them with some soy sauce.

Step 4: Final Assembly (5 minutes)

We packed the rice in a container and put the stir-fried chicken and vegetables in another container. We also prepared some green tea to drink.
